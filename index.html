ArgsBaseLab

AI for reasoning, critical thinking, and society

ArgsBase Lab studies and builds language technologies that help people and AI reason more critically about complex issues. Our work connects human–AI reasoning, deliberation, computational argumentation, multilingual AI, and societal NLP.
